Usage Guidelines for Weather App

The guide will help you understand how to use the app and what to expect from its features.

What the App does

The weather App Provide live weather update for any city you search.

  • Temperature(Current,Minimum,and Maximum)
  • Humidity info (hummidity percentage,wind direction,and "feels like" Temperature)
  • Wind info(Wind Speed,Sunrise and Sunset Time)

How to use the App

    1.Search The city

  • Type the city name in the "search the city" input bar
  • Press Enter or Click the Search Icon
  • The Weather Info Will update instantly for the selected city

    2.Reading The Data:

  • Temperature Card Show current Temperature,min/max values
  • Humidity Info:Display humidity % wind direction,and how the Temperature feels.
  • Wind Info Provide wind speed, sunrise and sunset times

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Always ensure you have an active internet connection.
  • Make sure city names are spelled correctly.
  • The weather data is fetched in real-time, so allow a few seconds for updates.

Important Note

  • This app uses third-party weather APIs, so minor delays or inaccuracies might occur due to network or data provider issues.
  • Weather can change rapidly; please do not rely on this app for critical safety decisions (like during storms or emergencies).
